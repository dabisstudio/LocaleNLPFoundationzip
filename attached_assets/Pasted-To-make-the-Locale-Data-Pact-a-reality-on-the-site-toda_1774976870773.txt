### 2️⃣ The Legal/Policy Backbone: The Locale Data Pact
Pass this to your legal counsel and grant writers. This is the executive summary of your Terms of Service. It transforms the concept of "ethical AI" into a binding, unassailable operational framework.

> #### **THE LOCALE DATA PACT: Sovereign Licensing Framework v1.0**
> 
> **Preamble:**
> The LocaleNLP Foundation recognizes that linguistic data generated by African and Arabic-speaking communities is a sovereign asset. Traditional data extraction methods (web scraping) violate the moral rights of the creators and introduce structural bias. The Locale Data Pact establishes a definitive, enforceable framework to ensure data sovereignty, equitable compensation, and frictionless open-source innovation.
> 
> **Article I: Contributor Sovereignty & The Lughatna Protocol**
> 1.  **Explicit Consent:** No data enters the LocaleNLP ecosystem without explicit, revocable, opt-in consent from the native speaker. 
> 2.  **Cryptographic Anonymity:** All voice and text contributions are stripped of Personally Identifiable Information (PII) at the edge and assigned a secure cryptographic hash before entering the central vault.
> 3.  **Direct Compensation:** Contributors maintain economic rights to their effort. A mandatory minimum percentage of all foundation commercial revenue is distributed directly to active contributors via integrated mobile money API gateways.
> 
> **Article II: The Academic & Public Good Tier (Free Access)**
> 1.  **Zero-Cost Infrastructure:** Verified academic institutions, non-profit organizations, and independent researchers operating in the public interest are granted full, zero-cost API access to foundational datasets.
> 2.  **The Open-Source Mandate:** Any derivative models, applications, or research published using the Academic Tier must be released under an open-source license (e.g., CC-BY-4.0 or MIT).
> 
> **Article III: Enterprise Commercial Licensing (The Sustainability Engine)**
> 1.  **Proprietary Use:** For-profit entities training closed-source, commercial Large Language Models (LLMs) must procure an Enterprise Commercial License.
> 2.  **Volume-Based Funding:** Enterprise access is billed volumetrically (per token or per speech hour). 
> 3.  **The Reinvestment Clause:** 100% of Enterprise licensing revenue is legally ring-fenced to cover core LocaleNLP operations, server compute costs, and the Lughatna Contributor Compensation Pool.
> 
> **Article IV: Governance & Auditing**
> The LocaleNLP Foundation submits to an annual, third-party algorithmic and financial audit to verify demographic parity across all datasets and confirm the transparent routing of commercial licensing fees back to local communities.

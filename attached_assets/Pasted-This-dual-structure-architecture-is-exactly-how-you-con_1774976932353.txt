### 1️⃣ THE FINANCIAL BRIDGE: The "Data Equity Agreement"

You need a formalized agreement between LocaleNLP (For-Profit) and LocaleNLP Foundation (Non-Profit). This document proves to VC investors that the For-Profit has a defensible data moat, and it proves to Foundation donors (like Mozilla) that the For-Profit isn't just exploiting the charity.

**The Core Mechanics of the Agreement:**

* **IP Ownership & Licensing:** The Foundation owns the raw, community-validated datasets (The Sovereign Asset). The Foundation grants the For-Profit an *exclusive commercial license* to monetize this specific data pipeline for enterprise use.
* **The Royalty Structure (The Flywheel):** In exchange for this commercial license, the For-Profit agrees to pay a "Compute & Community Subsidy" back to the Foundation. This is typically structured as **10% to 15% of gross API licensing revenue**. 
* **The Open-Source Mandate:** The agreement dictates that while the For-Profit builds proprietary, high-performance enterprise models, the Foundation retains the right to release "Baseline Models" and core datasets to the public for free, ensuring the public good is always served.

**Why this is a masterpiece for fundraising:** When you pitch the Foundation, you say: *"Your grant builds the initial data. Our For-Profit arm commercializes it, and the royalties guarantee the Foundation never runs out of money to pay local annotators."*

### 2️⃣ SPLITTING THE DIGITAL IDENTITY (The Two Websites)

If the Foundation and the For-Profit have the exact same website copy and design, you will confuse both donors and enterprise buyers. They need completely different digital psychological triggers.

#### A. LocaleNLP Foundation (The Non-Profit)
* **The Vibe:** *Circula.* Bright, frictionless, optimistic, human-centric, massive whitespace, soft shadows.
* **The Target Audience:** Institutional Philanthropy, Government Ministries, Academic Researchers, and Lughatna Contributors (native speakers).
* **The Core Value Proposition:** "Language is a human right. We are building the sovereign, community-owned digital infrastructure to ensure no voice is left behind in the AI era."
* **The Hero Image:** Human-centric UI. A floating mobile frame showing a local contributor validating audio on Lughatna, next to a glowing "Payout Sent" notification.
* **The Primary CTAs:** `Fund a Language Bounty` | `Access Open Datasets` | `Join as a Contributor`
* **The Proof Metrics:** Lives impacted, hours of data collected, percentage of female contributors, languages preserved.

#### B. LocaleNLP (The For-Profit)
* **The Vibe:** *AUI.io / Vercel / Stripe.* Dark mode, deep midnight backgrounds, neon glowing accents (cyan/ochre), monospaced typography, hyper-technical, and aggressive.
* **The Target Audience:** Enterprise CTOs, Telecom Executives, FinTech Founders, AI Researchers, and Venture Capitalists.
* **The Core Value Proposition:** "Enterprise-grade African & Arabic NLP. Deploy culturally native, hallucination-resistant models with millisecond latency."
* **The Hero Image:** Code-centric. A dark terminal window typing out a flawless Python request to the LocaleNLP API, translating a complex medical or financial prompt from English to Wolof in real-time.
* **The Primary CTAs:** `Get API Keys` | `View Documentation` | `Contact Enterprise Sales`
* **The Proof Metrics:** API uptime (99.99%), latency (ms), parameter counts, SOC2 compliance status, and enterprise SLA guarantees.

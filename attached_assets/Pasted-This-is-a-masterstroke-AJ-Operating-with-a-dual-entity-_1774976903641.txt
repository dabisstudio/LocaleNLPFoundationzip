### 1️⃣ THE DUAL-ENTITY SYMBIOSIS (How they interact)

You must establish a clean, legally unassailable "firewall" between the two entities, while allowing capital and data to flow through specific, defined gateways. 

**The Foundation (The Guardian & The Collector)**
* **The Role:** It owns the ethics, the community, the policy, and the open-source mission. 
* **The Assets:** It runs the *Lughatna* platform, manages the contributor payouts, and holds the raw, anonymized community datasets.
* **The Funding:** It accepts philanthropic grants, government funding, and tax-deductible donations. 

**The For-Profit (The Engine & The Commercializer)**
* **The Role:** It handles high-volume API infrastructure, proprietary fine-tuning, B2B sales, and enterprise SLAs (Service Level Agreements).
* **The Assets:** It holds the commercial technology stack, the enterprise client contracts, and the specialized commercial models.
* **The Funding:** It raises Venture Capital (VC) and generates high-margin SaaS/API revenue.

**The Financial Bridge (The Magic):**
The For-Profit pays a legally binding "Data Licensing Royalty" or "Compute Subsidy" *to* the Foundation. This means every time the For-Profit lands a massive contract with a bank or telecom, a percentage of that revenue flows directly back into the Foundation to fund more language collection.
